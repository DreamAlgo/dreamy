FAQs

Let's answer some questions.

How will I receive my algorithm notifications?

After signing up and completing your investing quiz, you will receive a link to join a Telegram channel. A web portal is coming soon if desired but most users are content with receiving alerts through their phone. Each notification message will include detailed information on the ticker, the time of the trade, and the execution price.

Is using Dream Algo safe?

Dream Algo is secured using the most modern technology, primarily 256-bit SSL encryption, to keep your data safe. We will never share your investing quiz responses with any third parties and if you decide Dream Algo isn't for you, we will remove them from our secure databases. We're committed to keeping your data safe.

How do I get support?

It's easy to get support from the Dream Algo team; just email support@dreamalgo.com. Our support team is available 24/7 to handle any question or issue from taking your investing quiz to receiving your alerts.
